Conference paper: Cooling in situations

Abstract

        This paper is to show the utility of multiple appliances that are used to decrease the temperature of a wanted target. It will showcase the physics behind the way it works and the reason why it is used in such a
        way. The three main cooling appliances will be about air conditioners, refridgerators and freezers.
